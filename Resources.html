CONTACT TINY TOTS STORE

You can order easily using our online platform. When you find a product you need, you can add it to cart, login and go through the ordering process. After the order is ready, you will receive order summary to your email. Order summary will also be stored to your account.
You can also easily make reorders afterwards by clicking the “reorder” button on any of your previously made orders. After clicking the “reorder” button the cart will open and you can change quantities or products.

If you want to cancel your order, please do so as soon as possible. If we have already processed your order, you need to contact us and return the product. Please contact trungntts2110033@fpt.edu.vn.

If you have question regarding our online store (ordering, account questions, technical questions), please contact annhbts2110010@fpt.edu.vn.
On pricing and shipping related issues you can contact customer support at trungntts2110033@fpt.edu.vn or call (+91) 123.4567890.

Go to this page Login and click “create a new account”, then just fill in all the needed information and click “create”. After submitting the form, your account will be confirmed and you will be notified.

You can retrieve your password by clicking “forgot password?”. Instruction on password retrieval will be send to your email.

You can easily change all your information on your account. Go to login page Login and log in, then click “my account” and “edit”. Here you can change all your contact information.

We will send you the tracking code of the shipment when the parcel has been sent.

Shipping costs are dependent on your location and products on your order. Some products need to be shipped in dry ice. These dry ice shipments have a slightly higher shipping fee. Our online store shows the shipping fee and shipping cost automatically on the checkout.

You can easily change your shipping address on your account. You just need to login and click “my account” and “edit”. You can also change the shipping address during the checkout process if you need. Just click “edit” below “shipping address”.
