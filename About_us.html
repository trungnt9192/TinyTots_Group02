About us


We opened Tinytots Store in November 2011 after leaving a small market stall, since opening we have become a stockist of Cosatto, O'baby, Venicci, Mee-go, Hauck, Didofy, ABC Design, Ickle Bubba and many more.

We stock everything you would need for the arrival of a new bundle of joy from prams, furniture, clothes all the way to the small items like feeding equipment. Also the toys we stock are from birth up to 8+ years. There is something for all age groups.

If there is anything that you cannot find please feel free to contact me and we will see what we can do, please keep checking our site as we are adding new items all the time and lots off offers as well.
